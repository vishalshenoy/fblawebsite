What are Projects?

Projects are a collaborative effort between FBLA members that range in topics and teach FBLA's principles.

American Enterprise

Participants develop projects within the school and/or community that increase the understanding of and support for the American enterprise system by developing an educational program

Middle Level

Participants help middle schoolers sharpen their communication, leadership, and teamwork skills, preparing them for high school and the workplace before many of their peers.

Individual Projects

Participants learn about many business-related topics that are explored throughout the year.

Community Service

Participants successfully implement community service projects to serve the citizens of their communities..

Partnership with Business

Participants learn about a business through communication and interaction with their local business community.
